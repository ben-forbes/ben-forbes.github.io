About Me

What’s up, I’m Ben!

I am a graduate student from Fresno, California completing a combined Bachelors and Masters degree in Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University. As a mid-distance freestyler on Northwestern’s Swimming and Diving team, I know how to make waves. My ability to grind out hard work in the pool is immediately transferable to my desire to enact social change as an engineer.

I am a problem solver and designer, who specializes in robotics and is relentless in the pursuit of understanding. In my ambition to change the world with my designs, I have streamlined my capabilities through exploration of law and political science.

Outside of work, school, and swimming, you can find me outside basking in the sun, eating delicious food, cheering for any Chicago sports team, or finding new trails to explore.

I am always ready to dive in, so feel free to contact me!
